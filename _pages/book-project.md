## Electoral Politics in Kuwait
*Book manuscript in progress*

This book project examines the politics of electoral competition in Kuwait, one of the Middle East's most institutionally distinctive political systems. Drawing on two original datasets — the **Kuwait National Assembly Elections Dataset (KNAED)** and the **Kuwait National Assembly Roll Call Votes Dataset (KNA-RCV)** — I trace how ethnic identity, ideology, and opposition strategy shape political competition across more than fifty years of elections.
