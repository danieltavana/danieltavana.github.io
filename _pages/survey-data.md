## Iran Social Survey (ISS)

With [Kevan Harris](https://kevanharris.sites.ucla.edu/) (UCLA), I am a Principal Investigator on the **Iran Social Survey (ISS)**, a multi-wave nationally representative survey of Iranian households. The ISS is one of the most comprehensive surveys ever fielded in Iran on social stratification, political attitudes, religion, migration, and family life.
