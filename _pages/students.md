## Prospective students

If you are a prospective Ph.D. student interested in the comparative politics of the Middle East, authoritarian regimes, electoral behavior, or political methodology, I encourage you to apply to the [Penn State Department of Political Science](https://polisci.la.psu.edu/graduate-program/). Feel free to email me at [tavana@psu.edu](mailto:tavana@psu.edu) if you have questions about the program or my research.
